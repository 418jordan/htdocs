Barrel + BARC 24 Hour Challenge

Join us at Barrel as we embark on a 24-hour productivity marathon to help Brooklyn-based BARC get a new website and other cool stuff!

The Barrel 24-Hour Challenge brings together the Barrel team, volunteers, and a nonprofit partner to accomplish big goals in a span of 24 hours.

This will be our inaugural effort, and we're really excited to be working with Brooklyn Animal Resource Coalition (BARC). The event will take place at Barrel Office.

Meet Barc!

Established in 1987, BARC is a nonprofit, no-kill animal shelter. Dedicated to providing a safe haven for homeless animals, BARC finds permanent loving homes for cats and dogs while ensuring quality food, water, and care for those still waiting.

Barc Stats/

Barc has the capacity to hold 75 cats and 35 dogs.

Each year, they find homes for nearly 1000 animals.

As a no-kill shelter, Barc must turn away up to 10 animals a day.

The Project

Redesign the BARC website, making it a more effective tool for attracting potential adopters and donors.

We'll make it easy for BARC staff to keep the website fresh with new animal profiles and Happy Tails, the BARC version of success stories.

Poster, t-shirts, tote bags, and more!

In addition to the website, we'll be designing and making lots of goodies for BARC.

...did we mention we're doing it all in 24 hours?

Start: August 6, 9AM
End: August 7, 9AM

It'll cost BARC nothing.

Barrel and our friends will be covering the entire cost of the project, an opportunity worth $20,000.
